<p class="weekly-intro">This week's tape was defined by two colliding forces: persistent institutional outflows from bitcoin products and a steady accumulation of structural infrastructure that will matter long after the drawdown ends. The macro backdrop darkened mid-week as geopolitical shocks compounded an already soft market, then partially cleared by Friday when the SpaceX IPO landed and risk appetite ticked up. The more durable story, though, was regulatory and architectural — Japan reclassified crypto as a financial instrument, the SEC signalled it may remove a key barrier to tokenised equity trading, and serious capital continued to flow into onchain credit and institutional settlement rails regardless of price action.</p>
