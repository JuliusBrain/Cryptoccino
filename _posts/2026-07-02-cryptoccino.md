## Bitcoin bounces off a 21-month low to $60K, but the hedges piling into $50K puts say the market isn't convinced.
{: #bitcoin-bounces-off-a-21-month-low-to-60k-but-the-hedges-piling-into-50k-puts-say-the-market-isnt-convinced}

[`coindesk`](https://www.coindesk.com/markets/2026/07/01/bitcoin-retakes-usd60-000-level-after-fed-chair-warsh-said-inflation-risks-has-come-down) [`decrypt`](https://decrypt.co/372522/bitcoin-pops-off-21-month-low-to-60k-as-soft-data-eases-rate-hike-fears) [`coindesk`](https://www.coindesk.com/markets/2026/07/01/bitcoin-options-traders-load-up-on-usd50-000-puts-and-gold-futures-flash-a-death-cross) [`coindesk`](https://www.coindesk.com/markets/2026/07/01/bitcoin-s-20-june-crash-looks-even-deadlier-on-the-charts-here-s-why)

<div class="fng-chip" aria-label="Crypto Fear &amp; Greed Index" title="Crypto Fear &amp; Greed Index. Scale 0 (extreme fear) to 100 (extreme greed)."><span class="fng-label">Fear &amp; Greed</span><span class="fng-value">19<span class="fng-scale">/100</span></span><span class="fng-class">Extreme Fear</span> <span class="fng-delta up">+7 / 7d</span></div>

**What happened.** Bitcoin dropped to a 21-month low before recovering to $60,000 after Fed Chair Warsh signalled that inflation risks have receded and softer US jobs and factory data revived rate-cut hopes. The bounce caps a June in which BTC shed roughly 20%, its worst first-half performance in years, and opens Q3 in what analysts describe as a historically hazardous chart zone.

**Why it matters.** Cantor Research is calling this the possible final stretch of the bear market, while Citi is moving in the opposite direction, cutting its 12-month BTC and ETH price targets as spot ETF flows logged their worst month on record, shedding $4.5 billion in June. The Fear &amp; Greed Index sits at 19, Extreme Fear, yet the 7-day delta is up seven points — a faint counter-signal that the capitulation may be finding a floor.

**The catch.** Options positioning tells a more defensive story: traders are building meaningful exposure to $50,000 puts, and gold futures flashing a death cross adds to the risk-off backdrop. Ark Invest bought over $75 million in crypto-related shares through the June selloff, including a fresh $18 million tranche of Circle stock, which reads either as conviction or as catching a falling knife depending on your view of the cycle.
